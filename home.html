One of London's most remarkeble landmark is London eye, this massive ride is only 5 minute walk from westminister underground station. It is in the south bank of the river thames. Londoy Bridge

London Bridge is an iconic and historical bridge that crosses the River thames and connects both part of the city. Everyday millions of tourist visits this amazing structure. This bridge represents London's long lasting heritage and this cities rich past and ongoing progress.
